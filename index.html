Historical Legacy

Chongqing's intangible cultural heritage boasts a long history, embodying the profound cultural heritage and traditional craftsmanship of the Bayu region.

Traditional craftsmanship

Spanning diverse fields such as handicrafts, folk culture, and traditional performances, it showcases a unique charm.

Cultural value

Intangible cultural heritage is not merely a matter of craftsmanship; it is a vital carrier of cultural memory and the national spirit.

Studio Profile

Our Mission

Dedicated to the preservation, transmission, and innovation of intangible cultural heritage, breathing new life into traditional craftsmanship.

Professional Team

Comprising inheritors of intangible cultural heritage and a design team, it blends tradition with modern creativity.

Cooperative Services

Provides diverse services such as cultural showcases, the development of cultural and creative products, and brand collaborations.
